國會現況

Political Cartoon about
Taiwan's Legislative Yuan

Poster
135 mm × 170 mm
2024/05/21

設計：陳盈誌
Design: Ying-Zhi Chen


2024 年 5 月，國民黨和民眾黨過半的國會，聯手強推立法院濫權法案。對此有感而發，創作此幅政治諷刺漫畫。首先使用 AI 工具將肖像和背景轉換成電玩遊戲「俠盜列車手Ｖ」的美術插畫風格，再合成至原圖上。

In May 2024, the KMT and TPP, with their combined majority in the Legislative Yuan, forcibly passed the terrible expansion of power bill. Inspired by this, I created this political satire artwork. First, I used an AI tool to convert portraits and background into the art style of the video game "GTA V," then composite it back onto the original image.


Font: Pricedown Black by Typodermic Fonts

背景：立法院議場（圖源：維基百科）
Background: Legislative Yuan Chamber (Wikipedia)



「我藐視」執事牌。執事牌演變自古代官員出巡時的手舉牌，用於提醒民眾官員的身分以及需要迴避、肅靜等，現在主要出現在神明出巡的隊伍中。「我藐視」執事牌呼籲人民勇於表達自己對國會現況的藐視。

The "I Contempt" plaque. The "執事牌" plaque evolved from the handheld signs used by officials during their inspections in ancient times, reminding people of the official's identity and the need for avoidance and silence. Today, they mainly appear in processions of deities. The "I Contempt" plaque calls on people to express their contemner for the current state of the Legislative Yuan.

台中林氏宗廟的執事牌（圖源：justfont）
"執事牌" in Lin Family Ancestral Shrine (justfont)
